Welcome to AudioLyt

AI-powered deepfake and autotuned audio detection

Real Audio

Real audio refers to recordings that have been captured naturally, without any manipulation.

Fake Audio

Fake audio refers to audio that has been synthetically generated or manipulated, often to mimic a specific voice or sound.

Autotuned Audio

Autotuned audio involves the use of software to correct or manipulate the pitch of a recording.

About Us

Audiolyt is an AI-driven solution designed to detect deepfake and autotuned audio, ensuring authenticity in voice recordings. Using advanced CNN and BiLSTM networks, it analyzes speech patterns and anomalies to differentiate between real and AI-generated voices. Trained on diverse datasets, AudioLyt helps combat voice fraud, misinformation, and unauthorized audio manipulation, making it a vital tool for media authentication, cybersecurity, and forensic applications.
